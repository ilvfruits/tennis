## Problem
The opponent constantly drop-shots and takes balls early.


## What Didn't Work
- Automatic lobs
- Soft replies


## What Works
- Deep crosscourt topspin
- Body shots
- Low dipping balls


## Key Lesson
> Depth first, pace second.
