## Recommended Tennis Books


### Winning Ugly – Brad Gilbert
**Focus:** Match strategy & mental toughness
**Why it matters:** Teaches how to win without playing perfect tennis.


---


### Essential Tennis – Ian Westermann
**Focus:** Skill development + practice systems
**Best for:** Players improving long-term consistency.


---


### Tennis Tactics – USTA
**Focus:** Patterns of play
**Best for:** Competitive juniors and tournament players.
